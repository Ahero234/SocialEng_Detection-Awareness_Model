Types of Social Engineering Attacks

Understanding common scams helps you stay one step ahead.

Phishing

Fake emails and messages designed to steal personal information.

Pretexting

Attackers create a false identity to trick victims into giving up sensitive data.

Baiting

Cybercriminals leave malware-infected media to lure victims into installing malicious software.
